This is a quiet spot on a trail in China’s Yellow Mountain park. We hiked there near the end of a long drought. The streams had shrunk to mere trickles and high thin clouds tantalized with promises of a rain that refused to come.
While enjoying the silence in that corner of the huge park, it was hard to believe that thousands were tramping their way up more popular trails. At the most famous peak crowds of hundreds of tourists sit on the ground, raptly listening to guides busy explaining the history of strange looking rocks and other objects that feature in the legends of China.
There are so many beautiful places tucked into this area that many famous films have been shot here. Pivitol scenes from Crouching Tiger Hidden Dragon were filmed just around the corner from Mindy’s impromtu seat.
